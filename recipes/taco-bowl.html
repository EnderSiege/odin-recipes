Taco Bowl

Description

No shell needed to enjoy tacos!

Ingredients

  • 1 lb Ground Beef
  • 3/4 cup water
  • Taco seasoning packet
  • 1/2 Avocado
  • Mexican shredded cheese
  • Sour Cream
  • Black Olives
  • Quest tortilla chips bag
  • Salsa

Steps

  1. Brown the ground beef in a skillet and drain.
  2. Mix 3/4 cup water and taco seasoning with the ground beef and raise temperature to boil, stir constantly.
  3. Once the ingredients are mixed and boil achieved, bring temperature to low for 10 minutes, stirring every few minutes.
  4. When the seasoned taco meat is finished (no more liquid), pour into a bowl.
  5. Open Quest tortilla chips bag, crush the chips into smaller pieces, then mix into the meat
  6. Top meat with sliced avocado, shredded cheese, sour cream, olives, and salsa
  7. Enjoy your delicious taco bowl!
