Burger Bowl

Description

No shell needed to enjoy tacos!

Ingredients

  • 1 lb Ground Beef
  • 1/2 Avocado
  • 2 slices of American Cheese
  • 1/2 cup Mayonaise
  • Mustard to taste
  • Salt to taste
  • Pickles as desired

Steps

  1. Brown the ground beef in a skillet and drain.
  2. Place the cooked ground beef in a bowl and mix with the mayonaise and salt.
  3. Place the slices of american cheese over the ground beef.
  4. Cover with sliced avocado, mustard, and pickes.
  5. Enjoy your delicious burger bowl!
