Pizza Skillet

Description

A pizza-lover's recipe minus the crust.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb Ground Beef
  • 1 cup pizza sauce
  • Sliced pepperoni
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Sliced olives
  • Salt to taste

Steps

  1. Brown the ground beef in a skillet and drain.
  2. Lower skillet temperature to low.
  3. Salt the ground beef as desired.
  4. Place sliced pepperoni over the ground beef while it's in the skillet.
  5. Cover with mozzeralla cheese and black olives.
  6. Close the skillet and let it cook for about 3 more minutes.
  7. Turn off the burner and let the pizza skillet settle.
  8. Enjoy your delicious burger bowl!
